Machu Picchu

As we all know, Machu Picchu is on of the seven wonders of the modern world. It is located in Peru, in the city of Cuzco. The reason why I want to visit this tourist place is because I am Peruvian and I have lived more than 24 years in Peru but I never had the opportunity to visit this majestic place. Other reason why I want to visit this place is to see the twelve angle stone in one of the walls of Machu Picchu.
