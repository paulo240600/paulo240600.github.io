One piece is a Shonnen category anime and manga written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew. Their main goal is to find the One Piece treasure and become the King of the Pirates.

In my opinion, One piece is one of the best the best manga and anime ever written. The story is full of diferent references about many cultures, movies and series. Eichiro Oda, the creator of the manga, used to expend a lot of time as kid reading books and watching movies that served as inspiration for his work.

Some people might argue that the manga is too long since it has more than 1100 chapters. Nonetheless, it is a story that you conenect with from the first chapters. In addition, as the GOAT Eiichiro Oda said The part that im most excited to do is the Final Saga.
