In order to venture to El Parque de las Leyendas, often referred as 'Park of Legends', from the United States, you will need to travel to Lima, and set down the address:

    Av. De los Poetas s/n, Miraflores 15074, Lima, Peru

The parking space in the park is quite numerous, we have 100 parkings spots in total, per the popularity of the attractions. There are both free and paid parking slots, with an addition of disabled slots. Tour guides and school slots are also available with prior reservation, offering a 20% discount for schools.
